Overview

Explore the Switzerland of India — "Kashmir Valley" — alongside popular heritage sites. Experience nature and culture together in one seamless journey. Enjoy a Shikara ride, visit Aru and Betaab Valleys, take a pony ride, spend 8 minutes on a ropeway, and relax in the Mughal Gardens.
