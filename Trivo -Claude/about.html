Our Story

From a tiny desk in Kerala to 80 countries

Trivo World started in 2015 when three friends returned from a backpacking trip across Southeast Asia and realised the biggest challenge wasn't money or time — it was the chaos of planning. Flight windows, hotel mismatches, missed local gems. They knew there had to be a smarter way.

What began as a small booking desk in Kochi has grown into a full-service travel company trusted by families, honeymooners, solo adventurers, and corporate teams across India. We don't just arrange trips — we design experiences that become the stories people tell for years.

Every itinerary we craft carries the same question: what would make this unforgettable? That question is still the heart of everything we do.
